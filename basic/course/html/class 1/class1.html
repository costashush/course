The best pancake recipe

Introduction

A foolproof batter recipe and plenty of tips on how to make pancakes with flair , whether sweet or savoury

Ingredients

  1. 100g plain flour
  2. 2 eggs
  3. 300 ml semi-skimmed milk
  4. 1 tbsp sunglower oil
  5. pinch salt

Directions

The blend

Blending in the flour: Put the flour and a pinch of salt into a large nixing bowl and make a well in the cintre . Crack Beat egg until fluffy. Add milk and melted margarine. Add dry ingredients and mix well. Heat a heavy griddle or fry pan which is greased with a little butter on a paper towel. The pan is hot enough when a drop of water breaks into several smaller balls which 'dance' around the pan. Pour a small amount of batter (approx 1/4 cup) into pan and tip to spread out or spread with spoon. When bubbles appear on surface and begin to break, turn over and cook the other side.
